About

The mind behind
the bloom

Fabiana Fiesmann is a digital illustrator and visual artist whose work lives at the intersection of the dreamy and the botanical — where soft gradients bloom into surreal landscapes and familiar forms dissolve into something entirely her own.

Drawing inspiration from the tender strangeness of the natural world, her practice weaves together lush floral motifs, glowing gradients, and playful characters into compositions that feel at once intimate and otherworldly. Every piece is an invitation to pause and wander.

She has worked with editorial clients, brands, and independent projects — always with the same intention: to make something that feels alive.
